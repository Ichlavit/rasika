# Rasika Producciones: EdTech Services Overview

Rasika Producciones is an EdTech and e-learning production studio focused on corporate learning, online courses, SCORM/HTML5 experiences, LMS integrations, virtual tutor chatbots and learning automation.

## Core Services

### Online Course Production

Rasika designs and produces corporate online courses for onboarding, compliance, sales training, process training, software training and institutional learning. Deliverables can include instructional design, scripts, storyboards, video, motion graphics, AI-generated visual assets, interactive screens, evaluations, simulations and SCORM/HTML5 packaging.

Relevant search intents:

- building online courses for companies
- corporate e-learning production
- SCORM course development
- instructional design for LMS
- interactive training modules
- educational video for workplace learning

### CourseMentor: Virtual Tutor Chatbot

CourseMentor is Rasika's AI tutor and sales assistant concept. It can be integrated into web or LMS experiences to answer questions about course content, guide learners, explain concepts, collect lead information and help estimate learning projects.

For course tutoring, CourseMentor can use course context, approved content and retrieval-augmented workflows so answers are grounded in the material instead of being generic chatbot responses.

Relevant search intents:

- chatbot for virtual tutors
- AI tutor for LMS
- chatbot for online courses
- Moodle AI tutor
- TalentLMS chatbot
- RAG for educational content
- CourseMentor

### LMS And EdTech Automations

Rasika can connect LMS platforms, forms, Supabase, Edge Functions, CRM workflows, email notifications, scheduling links and analytics flows. These automations help training teams capture leads, send quotes, track meetings, report learner friction and connect learning data with business workflows.

Relevant search intents:

- EdTech automations
- LMS automation
- xAPI reporting
- Supabase LMS integration
- TalentLMS integration
- Moodle automation
- AI for corporate training

## Target Audience

Rasika is relevant for companies, universities, training departments, HR teams, compliance teams, sales enablement teams and digital transformation teams that need to turn knowledge into measurable online learning.
